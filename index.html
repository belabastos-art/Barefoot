Barefoot

BAREFOOT IS A SENSORIAL WALKING EXPERIENCE TO REDISCOVER THE CITY THROUGH ATTENTION AND TOUCH. Informed by the intimacy of walking barefoot, it facilitates urban spaces to become sites of presence, memory, and connection.

FOLLOWING A CURATED PATH OF FIVE BAREFOOT FRIENDLY LOCATIONS ACROSS THE CITY, participants are prompted to engage directly with the living surfaces of grass, wood and earth to break the barrier between body and environment, tracing a path where the city and the self meet at skin level.

FOR EACH PASSAGE, AN AUDIO PROMPT ANCHORS THE EXPERIENCE, BUT THERE IS NO LINEAR NARRATIVE. BAREFOOT is an invitation to walk slowly & to feel the textures. It may be experienced solo or shared, analog or digital, improvised or guided. No two walks are ever the same.
